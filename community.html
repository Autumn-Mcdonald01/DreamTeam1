Community Involvement

Students from ECH, both in National Honor Society and not, are constantly recognized for their various achievements and
contributions. Chapter membership acknowledges student activities, encouraging them to develop further through active involvement in
school activities and community service.

ECH Students Recognized for their Achievements

The Gamma Alpha Omega chapter of Alpha Kappa Alpha recognized local students
during their Founders Day celebration on Sunday, Febuary 16th. Three of ECH's
students were recognized for Academics, Arts, and Community Service.

County Chapters Gather Together

To celebrate the community service performed by Madison County chapters,
all members were invited to dinner and an awards ceremony. Donning official
robes, students were awarded based on their representation of the four pillars
of NHS; Scholarship, Leadership, Service, and Character.
